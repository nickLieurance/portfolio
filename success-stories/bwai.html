How I helped SurveyMonkey use AI to make it easy for anyone to create a survey

Overview

  • Company: SurveyMonkey
  • Product: Build with AI
  • Platform: Web
  • Timeline: ~6 months
  • My contributions: AI prompt structure, feature name, terminology, & UI Copy
  • Description: Build with AI is a new feature that creates a survey based on short instructions from the user (a prompt).

Audience

Build with AI is for all survey authors, novices and experts alike. Even people on free plans can use it, although they need to upgrade if they want to include premium question types in their surveys.

Problem

It's hard to write a good survey. You need strong writing skills. You have to know what questions to ask and in what order. Even if you can do all that, it takes a long time to write a survey. How could we use AI to make the process easier for users of all levels?

My solution

I analyzed competitors to identify content needs and opportunities. What I found was that it's not easy to write a good prompt. Most competitors solved this problem by offering sample prompts. The designer and I spoke about the challenge and agreed on a similar solution. When the designer showed me an initial concept based on our discussion, I decided to add a bit of step-by-step guidance. Then we worked together to refine the design and gather feedback from stakeholders. When we had our final design, I ran a usability study to make sure it felt intuitive to users. To choose the feature name, I ran a workshop where cross-functional stakeholders aligned on criteria, brainstormed, and shortlisted three names. Then I surveyed 200 people to determine the winning name.
